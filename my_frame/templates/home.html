FAQ

To build a MyFrame digital art frame you will need the following:

  • Internet connection
  • A Raspberry Pi
  • A mouse and keyboard to connect to your Pi
  • Computer monitor or LED screen with USB ports.

This project is completely free besides the hardware. The cost of hardware can vary greatly, especially if you purchase new. We were able to put one together for under $150!
